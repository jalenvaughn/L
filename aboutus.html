About Us

We are an energy drink company that strays away from the traditional energy drink that most consumers know. We don't offer beverages that have intense supplements added or even ingredients that are hard to pronounce. We instead only include fruit juice mixes, coconut water, a pinch of caffeine derieved from green tea leaves, and vegtable juices. These ingredients keep the body happy, while energzing the mind just the right amount.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a clean and natural energy boost that supports both your body and mind. We strive to have the best tasting and healthy energy drink to reach the market, in hopes of reducing the harsh accidents caused by over-consumption of caffeine from other energy-related beverages.
